# Product Brief

## Product

Control Library

## Problem

Teams need a controlled way to convert messy uploaded documents into consistent technical documentation without losing source intent, constraints, assumptions, or reviewability.

## Users

- Technical users who need architecture, integration, implementation, and operational documentation.
- Functional users who provide source material and business context.
- Reviewers who validate generated documentation before it becomes official.

## Near-Term Outcome

A user uploads a source document. The system detects whether the user intends technical documentation, extracts relevant context, selects the technical documentation template, and creates a cloned copy populated with source-grounded content.

## Future Outcome

The same controlled environment can add new skills such as functional documentation, peer review, Sora deck creation, and artifact-specific publishing without rewriting the core intake and governance model.
